About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 focuses on combating rising urban temperatures and frequent flash floods. Students, developers, and community innovators will collaborate to build digital solutions that advance SDG 13: Climate Action.

  • Mitigate urban heat through smart cooling and green infrastructure.
  • Improve flood resilience with early-warning and response systems.
  • Raise climate awareness using data visualization and storytelling.

SDG 13 — Climate Action

This event supports global efforts to reduce climate risks through innovation and collaboration. Participants’ projects will focus on:

  • Early-warning systems for flood and heat risks.
  • Mapping green infrastructure to cool cities.
  • Raising awareness through digital storytelling tools.
